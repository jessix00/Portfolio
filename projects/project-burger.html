The Project

I wanted to familiarize myself with Adobe XD and the Foundation framework so I decided to build a simple restaurant website.

The Wireframes

I started by creating the wireframes in Adobe XD. I wanted the site flow to be simple and to the point so I decided to keep everything on one page.

The Mockup

The design was inspired by retro diners. I chose a very strong font for my headers and the colors red (which are a theme among fast food restaurants), black and white. I put a checkered border on the main image to reference the retro linoleum checkered floors many diners had in the 50â€™s.
