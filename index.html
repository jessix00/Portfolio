Hello my name is Jessica, a Web Developer & Designer living in Phoenix, AZ.

About

I come from a diverse background but I’ve always been interested in design and technology. As a teenager, I was always involved in the arts. I enjoyed expressing myself through acrylic painting, digital photography and graphic design.

In 2015, I became interested in web development and I began to learn HTML and CSS. This lead me to join a web development bootcamp where I was introduced to JavaScript and other developer tools.

At the moment, I’m doing a lot of self studying from sites like Udacity and Udemy. I still find that the best lessons are learned from completing real-world projects and for this reason, I’m always looking for ways to build applications and websites.

When it comes to work, I want to be involved in projects I find challenging with people that inspire me. Since programming has been such a big part of my life, I enjoy meeting others that share my passion.
