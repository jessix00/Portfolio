Hello my name is Jessica, a Web Designer & Developer living in Phoenix, AZ.

About

I’m a desert native who’s lived in Phoenix, Arizona most of her life. Although I come from a diverse background, I’ve always had an interest in design and technology. In 2015, I taught myself HTML5 and CSS, this lead me to join a web development bootcamp where I continued to learn and grow as a developer.

A lot of the studying I currently do is from web tutorials and videos. I still find the best lessons are learned from completing real-world projects and for this reason, I’m always looking for ways to improve and build websites.

When it comes to work, I want to be involved in projects I find challenging with people that inspire me. Since programming has been such a big part of my life, I enjoy meeting others that share my passion.
